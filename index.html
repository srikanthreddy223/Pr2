Kadapa is a city in the southern part of Andhra pradesh, India. It is located in Rayalaseema region, and is the district headquaters of YSR kadapa district. It is located 8 kilometer south of the Penna River. The city is surrounded on three sides by the Nallamala and Palkonda Hills lying.
